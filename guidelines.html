How to order?

  1. Customers can connect with us over either a phone call, email, or Whatsapp, and discuss the availability of the product (please keep the product code handy, it makes the process quick!).
  2. We will respond back confirming availability of the product, and payment details should it be in stock.
  3. Upon receipt of the payment, we will execute the order.

Product availability

  1. We have a limited stock given how it takes time for the weavers to make the sarees.
  2. As such, though we will keep our catalog updated as much as possible, please understand that we might not have all the sarees in stock.

Pricing

While we take all effort to ensure accuracy of information provided on the website, there could be human error in either the details provided or the price of a product. In such cases, we reserve the sole right to refuse the order and will let the customer know about the issue and send corrected information.

Payment modes

We accept payments via:
  1. UPI payments
  2. Bank NEFTs
Please get in touch with us to find out more.

Delivery and Shipping

  1. Customer can opt for India Post Parcel service and we will ship it accordingly.
  2. Customers are requested not to accept damaged/tampered packages. We take tampering seriously, so please get back to us.

I received a damaged or the wrong product, what do I do?

While utmost care is taken to ensure products reach the customers safely, there could be instances of them getting damaged or mixed up during transport or elsewhere. In the event you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please follow the steps below:
  1. Please inform us about the damage within 24 hours of receipt of the product.
  2. Cases will be taken up on a case-to-case basis upon mutual understanding and acceptance. Reasonable analysis will be done and depending on the result, the customer can get a replacement or refund depending on the case.
  3. Customers are to return the defective product within 5 working days of receipt of the product.
We strive to make this a productive experience for you, while also making sure the weaver does not get shortchanged, so we seek your understanding in this matter.

Order cancellation

  1. We reserve the right to cancel or refuse an order. Reasons could include inaccurate product or pricing details. We will always communicate this with the customer.
  2. Customers can cancel their orders on the same day. However, If the order has shipped, we reserve the right to withhold the shipping charges. The customer has to send back the order as-is, unopened. The customer will bear the charges of returning the product. The difference in the amount will be reimbursed to the customer within 7 working days.

Ordering outside India

  1. We cater to orders placed from outside India.
  2. We only accept payment in Indian National Rupees.
  3. Shipping charges will be borne by the customer, and they can choose how it should be shipped.
  4. In this case, we shall not be responsible for any damage occuring during transit.
