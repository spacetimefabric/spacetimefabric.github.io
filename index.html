About Us

The name Markanda is synonymous with purity and honesty, and sarees have always been a symbol of tradition, elegance and dignity.

With Markanda, we connect you to the roots of Indian weaving heritage, the richness of which manifests itself the through the dexterity and expertise of our master weavers. They create sarees 5 meters long that could be folded to fit into a match box. We have embarked on this journey with the intention of supporting and encouraging the weavers to continue with their hereditary profession and earn a decent livelihood.
